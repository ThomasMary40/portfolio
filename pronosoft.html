Pronosoft


Find the best of Pronosoft in this 100% free application and intended for bettors who wish to prepare their forecasts with the maximum of information in hand!

We offer you our predictions on all the Loto Sports®, Loto Foot®, Loto Rugby® and Loto Basket® grids (registered trademarks of the FDJ®), as well as for all the matches of the ParionsSport® offer (registered trademark of the FDJ®).

Also consult the detailed analyzes of our tipsters as well as our daily forecast for Quinté+.

In addition to our predictions, you will appreciate the history and rankings of each team. You will thus prepare your games (in the form of validation Flashcodes) in the best possible way and you will be able to follow the results of your predictions in real time (in the application or via push notifications).

Dark mode has recently appeared to allow you greater visual comfort.
