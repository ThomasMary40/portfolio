Lectra Snapshot


Lectra Snapshot helps fashion and apparel companies stay on top of trends by allowing their employees to take photos and share their inspirations while on the go. Via this application, images can be annotated directly and saved on the Lectra Fashion PLM platform.

Users can thus share the photos captured instantly via Lectra Snapshot and organize them according to location, date, or theme. As a result, access, use and collaboration will be facilitated, with the aim of developing and making current collections available on the market as soon as possible.

With Lectra Snapshot, users can:
- Take a photograph from their mobile device and annotate it
- Create dedicated folders on the Platform via the application to organize their images
- Store images in Lectra Fashion PLM for later use.
- Share images with other team members.
