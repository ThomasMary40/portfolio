Lectra Collection Advanced


Lectra Collection Advanced allows those involved in the creation and development of fashion products to access the Lectra Fashion PLM product catalog on the go, to annotate and comment on product sheets and to share them with all teams.

The synthetic visualization of product specifications facilitates decision-making during collection reviews.

Lectra Collection Advanced allows you to access the product catalogs of your collections at any time, even when you are offline.

Lectra Collection offers the possibility of:
View Lectra Fashion PLM products
Create filters with specific product search criteria (by price, size, theme,..)
View the specifics and technical file of a selected product (reference, size, color, price, CAD, etc.)
Modify the visual of a product sheet
Consult a product catalog even when you are not connected to the platform (off-line mode)
Share your notes and comments on products with your whole team via the “change tracking” function
