Lectra Todo List


Lectra To Do List allows fashion product development professionals to continue managing and organizing Lectra Fashion PLM tasks on the go.

Visibility and control of activities are facilitated during business trips, accelerating product development and decision-making.

Lectra To Do List allows the user to:
- Consult a list of predefined tasks in Lectra Fashion PLM
- Filter tasks in a specific time period
- Identify tasks that are ahead or behind schedule
- Validate a task and update the plan
